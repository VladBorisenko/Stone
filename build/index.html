Steve White’s project
In the wild, stones like to walk

You have probably already seen similar photos, on which the movement of stones is clearly visible. At first glance, this seems unthinkable. After all, a block is the most motionless of what only exists on Earth. But you need to take a closer look at the stones. Then it turns out that not everything is so simple. You may not believe it, but stone sculptures grow old, breathe, think and even move.

How was it started
2021

Young and ambitions, Steve White, decided to assemble a team of experts to find out the reason for this behavior of stones, and to figure out

“why the stones do not walk in the city?”

6730

Interviewed

3,2%

Walk their stones

74%

Neglect the health of their stones

At the end of the study, we noticed a dependence of the life expectancy of the stones on the number of walks per day.

Based on the data we collected and analyzed, at the end of the qualitative and quantitative studies, which included 6743 reviewers, we can conclude that daily walks for the stones are very important and necessary, then the stones will live happily ever after.
